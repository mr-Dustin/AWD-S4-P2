Mang Inasal

Mang Inasal is the Philippines’ Grill Expert that delightfully serves Ihaw-Sarap food and Unli-Saya experience.

Family Size Palabok

Noodles in thick rich sauce seasoned with chopped spring onions, hard-boiled egg slices, shrimp, chicharon bits, meaty pork and tinapa.

Sizzling Sisig

A mouth-watering grilled and sautéed meaty dish with special dressing, chicharon, diced onion, sliced red & finger chili. Good for 3 to 4 people.

Chicken Quarter Leg

4 pieces Ihaw-Sarap marinated chicken quarters (Paa) expertly grilled to perfection.

Pork Barbecue

10 sticks of juicy-lambot Pork BBQ served with sweet and savory peanut sauce.
